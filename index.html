Below is a Table of all the essential videos. The rows represent a theme and the columns represent specific technique(s).

The rows are ordered top-down for your development. John Danaher has stated it is crucial to build your game ground-up as a beginner. You start with escapes, retention, move to half guard, full guard, until you are on your feet. Submission attacks can be learned during this process but come secondary to the positional game. Danaher only teaches experts submissions first, much like how experienced chess players study the end-game.
